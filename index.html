About

Saber Zou is a Design Team Lead at Microsoft, leading 70+ designers across AI, Search, Browser, and Content experiences — core products used by billions of people worldwide.

Based in Beijing, he works at the intersection of design leadership and emerging technology, shaping how people interact with AI-powered tools every day.

Outside of work, he explores street photography, draws simple sketches on iPad, and builds experimental projects that push the boundaries of what AI and design can do together.
